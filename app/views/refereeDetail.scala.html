Referee

  • Please give the name and email address of your academic referee, who is familiar with, and can comment on, your academic abilities.
  • The University will contact your referee directly by email as soon as you submit this form.
  • Please note the University will only accept academic recommendations received from an official institutional email account (references received from Yahoo, Gmail, Hotmail and other such accounts will not be acceptable) and all recommendations must be submitted in English.
  • Your referee will be asked to submit a reference directly to the University via our secure website. Please ensure that you've informed your referee that you're applying for further studies and that they have agreed to provide a recommendation for you. Please note that your application will be delayed if references are not received in good time and it is your responsibility to ensure that these are submitted.
  • If you cannot provide an academic reference, a professional one will suffice.
    We can only accept references from a verifiable email address. References submitted from a personal email address (e.g. Hotmail, Yahoo, gmail, etc.) will not be considered.

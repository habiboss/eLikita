You might get advanced entry to a degree course if you have certified learning at Higher Education level in a subject relevant to what you want to study.

When you have credit awarded like this it�s called accredited prior learning (APL).

If you want us to look at your previous qualifications to see if you qualify for APL, you have to provide an official transcript of your results from the institution where you gained your qualification and module guides of all the modules you want considered and we�ll then see if your prior learning applies to the course you want to join. All learning must have been done in the last five years.

This is when you want to start your course. Credit is given for accredited study at the right level that�s specific to the course. We�ll also assess your prior learning to make sure it�s in line with what you�ll learn on the course you want to join.
